The Magic Dolphin

According to Rosaline Lee Fung, the creator of Phinnie, this magic dolphin is the beloved storyteller of the world. Born in the South China Sea, he was kissed by the moon on his first leap out of the water. Among the many gifts from the moon, Phinnie has eternal youth. He has the wisdom of the ages and the charm of a little boy. He befriends children of the world by sharing with them stories everywhere he goes.

The Statue

Since the year 2002, Phinnie has been immortalized by the famous sculptor Van Lau, and the Phinnie statue towers 5 meters high on the Ma Wan Beach in Hong Kong. The statue of Phinnie is fast becoming a tourist attraction for school children and their parents who are familiar with the Read with Phinnie storybooks published by Oxford University Press.


The Phinnie Project

The Phinnie project is twenty years in the making. Currently there are over 130 Phinnie storybooks and close to 20 Phinnie Learning Centers in China.
